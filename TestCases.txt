Question: Capital of India
Answer: The capital of India is New Delhi. New Delhi is located in the northern part of India and is the seat of the national government. It is also the capital of the National Capital Territory of Delhi, which is a union territory within India. The city was officially declared the capital of India in 1911, after the British government decided to move the capital from Calcutta (now Kolkata) to Delhi. New Delhi is a bustling metropolis with a population of over 22 million people. It is known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and modern infrastructure.

Question: Where is taj mahal located ?
Answer: The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, a city in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Agra is located about 200 miles south of New Delhi, the capital of India. The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous landmarks in India and is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is a mausoleum that was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal, who died during childbirth in 1631. The Taj Mahal is considered one of the finest examples of Mughal architecture in the world and is visited by millions of tourists each year.
